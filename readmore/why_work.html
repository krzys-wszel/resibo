Unique Effective Ingredients

Lipout™

The single celled Tisochrysis lutea alga extract transforms fat cells into the cells burning fat and speeds up the thermogenesis process. The effect is a visibly slimmer and slenderer silhouette, as well as a firmer and healthier skin.

PROVEN RESULTS – Following the researchers’ results after 2 months of using it on critical areas (hips, abdomen, waist and thighs), every tested person noticed a size reduction. After 56 days an average reduction size of a thighs is 1.4 cm, hips 1.3 cm and abdomen 2.2 cm.

Stoechiol®

Butterfly lavender extract – Thanks to this extract you will obtain a natural effect, similar to applying a botulinum. The active ingredient works on the basis of a patented method to quickly get rid of wrinkles by inducing the release of beta-endorphins into the skin, in order to loosen it up and reduce wrinkles.

PROVEN RESULTS – 24 hours after the first application on the “crow’s feet”, testing group was women average age of 54. They noticed: a reduction in the depth of wrinkles by 17.4% and decrease in the roughness of skin by 12.7%

Soliberine®

Concentrated extract of Buddleja officinalis, this ingredient helps to maintain the proper physiology of the skin, protecting it from all types of the pollution, smog and harmful light rays, like UVA, UVB, IR or concentrated blue light and visible light. It protects the skin from inflammations, irritations and dryness, prevents from melasma and premature wrinkles.

PROVEN RESULTS – Reduction of free radicals on human keratinocytes, irritated with blue light from our computers, smartphones etc. is up to 43% after use Soliberine®, so this is a real protection shield.
