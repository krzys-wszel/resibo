Ewelina Kwit-Betlej the brand owner

our history.

Dear Friends,

Re.power your beauty with RESIBO – a unique, natural and 100% vegan cosmetic brand made in Poland.

Resibo offers line of a skincare and body care cosmetics, created out of love for beauty, nature and the environment.

The product I am most proud of - our Cleansing Oil, was invented by me as a result of a lifelong search for a solution to my skin problems. It has been a life changer for me and eventually many  of our clients who have claimed it to be their holy grail of cleansing products for problematic skin.

This was the beginning of Resibo's journey towards great skincare. Me and my team are constantly working on new innovative solutions.

Our main goal is to expand and keep looking for solutions, which will allow us to apply the highest possible amounts of concentrated natural extracts, obtained by a method using the most up-to-date technology. This is what makes Resibo unique and different from many other widely available cosmetics on the market.

Resibo products are on average 98.8% natural, proven to be highly effective and efficient.

As an eco-friendly company we work to create a conscious consumption, to educate ecologically and to be active in  a sustainable living movement. We believe in promoting female beauty, regardless of age or skin colour.

I originally created Resibo as an answer to my skin’s needs and therefore, naturally, the key values of the company include the importance of self-acceptance as well as a holistic approach  to beauty care. These values are particularly important to me and  I believe most women around the globe.

We are proud of our products and have had an amazing feedback from many clients – this is the best reward and satisfaction as well as proof that our products work and are loved by others.
